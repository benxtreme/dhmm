DAG HEWARD-MILLS • PRESIDING BISHOP OF UD-OLGC

Bishop Dag Heward-Mills

Dag Heward-Mills, un evangelista africano, es el fundador y supervisor general de las Denominaciones Unidas originarias del Grupo de Iglesias Lighthouse (UD-OLGC, anteriormente Lighthouse Chapel International). Como médico calificado, Dag se entregó por completo a la obra de Dios en 1991 y nunca miró hacia atrás. Dag Heward-Mills tiene un ministerio múltiple que ha transformado y continúa impactando a millones de vidas en todo el mundo.

El UD-OLGC tiene presencia en 94 países y en todos los continentes habitables. En más de tres décadas de ministerio, la pasión de Dag Heward-Mills por la obra de Dios se ha caracterizado por construir iglesias, entrenar pastores, ayudar a los necesitados e inválidos, crear, producir música, hablar en conferencias, pastorear jóvenes y realizar campañas masivas de evangelización. en muchas naciones

Fue recibido por muchos presidentes, jefes y líderes de naciones, y personalmente ha llevado a varios de ellos a Cristo. Dag Heward-Mills es muy admirado por su estilo de enseñanza y escritura sincero y realista.

Beginnings

Dag was born to Swiss mother Elisabeth and Ghanaian father Nathaniel Heward-Mills on May 14th, 1963. During his younger years, Dag enjoyed horse-riding and hanging out with friends.

Dag gave his life to Christ when he was about sixteen years old and under diligent mentorship, grew in the faith during his high school and university years.

He became a pastor at the age of twenty-five and was later formally ordained by Pastor Michael Bassett of the Victory Church in London, and subsequently consecrated as a Bishop by the International Ministerial Council of Great Britain.

“From today, you can teach…”

In 1988, during his final year of medical school, Dag was on a community health rotation program in a town called Suhum. Dag Heward-Mills (then Brother Dag) was praying in his room on his knees and resting his head on his bed, when he fell asleep.

He had left a message preached by Kenneth Hagin playing on repeat.

Dag later recounts, “At about 3 a.m., I was kneeling by the bed praying. I could see the tape recorder situated at the other end of the room. Then suddenly, something literally jumped out of the tape which was playing and moved into my belly.

I could feel it entering me. Then I heard a voice saying, “From today, you can teach.” Then I heard another voice, different to the first, that said, “I will prove it to you”. Upon his return to the church in Accra, Dag’s ministry began to change.

Today, we witness the tangible effects and undeniable proof of God’s calling on Bishop Dag Heward Mills’ life and the supernatural power behind those words, “From today, you can teach”.

ANAGKAZO BIBLE & MINISTRY TRAINING CENTRE

A significant part of Dag Heward-Mills’ ministry involves the training and nurturing of pastors. It is in this spirit he has founded the Anagkazo Bible & Ministry Training Center (ABMTC).

The ABMTC comprises an awe-inspiring state-of-the-art campus located in Mampong, Ghana. Many alumni of ABMTC have been known to advance far in ministry and build large churches.

The campus also houses the “Library Of The Anointing”, a museum which features life-sized effigies of prominent men of God from different times in history.

Conference Speaking

Dag Heward-Mills holds the Give Thyself Wholly Conference several times a year, in different countries of the world, where he teaches and trains pastors and church- workers for many hours on subjects such as Loyalty & Disloyalty, Church Growth, Shepherding, The Art of Leadership, and The Anointing.

There are a plethora of testimonies of senior pastors, assistant pastors, church leaders, and church workers whose lives and ministries have been transformed by attending these conferences.
